Synesthesia
Synesthesia is an intelligent recommendation system for classical paintings and classical music. It analyzes images or audio clips to suggest similar works within the same domain — allowing you to explore art and sound through patterns of mood, color, and tone.

Benefits
Artistic Understanding Synesthesia analyzes both paintings and classical music to uncover aesthetic similarities.
Cultural Discovery Dive into art and music collections to find connections across eras and emotional expression.
Intuitive Exploration Upload an image or a music clip and get instant recommendations of similar works.
Educational Value Ideal for students and enthusiasts exploring classical art and music relationships.
Use Cases
Synesthesia helps users explore connections within classical domains — find similar paintings or discover related musical compositions.
Painting Discovery Find classical artworks that resemble your uploaded painting in style or emotion.
Music Exploration Discover classical pieces that sound similar to your uploaded track.
